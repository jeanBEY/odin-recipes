Adobo

Description

Adobo is a delicious Filipino chicken dish in which the chicken is braised in soy sauce & vinegar. Sometimes pork is used instead of chicken.

Simmering it slow makes the chicken very tender and flavorful. The sauce can be poured over the rice as well.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Soy Sauce, 2 Spoons
  • Vinegar, 2.5 Spoons
  • Garlic
  • Bay Leaves
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Saute chopped garlic, wait until light brown.
  2. Add chicken and stir for about 5 minutes. Add spoon full of bay leaves and a lot of black pepper. 85-95% coat of black pepper.
  3. Add 2 cooking spoons of soy sauce while chicken is not entirely cooked yet. Close lid.
  4. Let it cook in soy sauce on high heat.
  5. When it starts to boil, lower the heat and cook without the lid. Add 2.5 cooking spoons of vinegar. Do not cover, stir occasionally.
  6. Do not need to add water unless it is too salty.
