Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic dish, made of up noodles and tomato sauce.

When you have the right blend of pepper, salt, basil and oregano; it creates the perfect blend of tangy, salty, and sweet.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato Sauce with Mushrooms -- Preggo is a good brand
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Saute chopped garlic, wait until light brown.
  2. Saute onions.
  3. Saute ground beef. Season with salt and pepper. Cover with lid on high heat until meat is cooked.
  4. After about 10-15 minutes, season with salt, pepper, basil and oregano to taste. Make sure to taste it after each small amount of seasoning.
  5. Once the flavor is to your liking, simmer on low heat for another 20-30 minutes.
