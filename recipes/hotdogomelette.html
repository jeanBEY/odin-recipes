Hot Dog Omelette

Description

It is exactly as it sounds! Sliced hot dog mixed with egg to create an omelette.

This was comfort food growing up as a kid, when you couldn't decide what you wanted to pair with rice, hot dog or egg.

Ingredients

  • Hot Dog
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Chop hot dog into small pieces.
  2. Mix egg in a bowl & pour into pan.
  3. Add chopped hot dogs into pan with egg.
  4. Wait until it can be flipped over, just like you would a pan cake. Or if you prefer, you can create more of scrambled egg dish and mix it in the pan.
